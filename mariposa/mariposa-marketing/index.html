Our Story

After living the challenges first hand of managing quality in-home care for my Mom who was diagnosed with Parkinsonism five years ago, I've been inspired to launch a solution that will enable seniors to live meaningful, connected and fulfilled lives in the safety & comfort of their own homes.

Particularly in light of the additional challenges brought about by the current pandemic, I’m thrilled to launch our free mariposa.care app with the hope that we will provide significant value for remote family members, caregivers and seniors needing assistance.

My goal is to simplify care coordination and enable family members to focus on what really matters; enabling their loved ones to live extraordinary lives and creating meaningful moments together!

By leveraging technology combined with a positive mindset, grounded in fearlessness, love, comfort, balance, peace and abundance, I truly believe we have the opportunity to transform senior living.

Jim Lightsey

Founder, Mariposa
