  3. After the policy contract is issued, the annuity option chosen and annuity payable is guaranteed for life or guaranteed term basis the annuity option chosen by you, and cannot be changed
  4. Annuity Options : There are 6 annuity options available under this plan as mentioned below:
    • Option 1: Lifetime Income : Annuity amount shall be payable throughout lifetime of the Annuitant ; On death of the Annuitant, the plan terminates
    • Option 2: Lifetime Income with Cash-Back : Annuity amount shall be payable throughout lifetime of the Annuitant ; On death of the Annuitant, Total Premium (excluding Goods and Services Tax and Other Statutory levies shall be payable to the Nominee / Legal Heir
    • Option 3: Lifetime Income with Term Guarantee : Annuity amount shall be payable for the guaranteed term (5/10/15/20 years) as opted by the Proposer on inception irrespective of the survival status of the Annuitant. In case the Annuitant survives the chosen guaranteed term then payout will continue throughout his / her lifetime and on death the annuity payout will cease. Upon death of the annuitant during the guaranteed term, the annuity payout to the nominee will continue till the expiry of the guaranteed term.
    • Option 4: Last survivor Lifetime Income with 100% annuity to Surviving Spouse : Annuity amount shall be payable throughout lifetime of the Primary Annuitant and upon his / her death 100% of the initial annuity payout will be made throughout the lifetime of the Secondary Annuitant / Surviving Spouse. If the Secondary Annuitant predeceases the Primary Annuitant, 100% of the annuity payouts shall continue for the Primary Annuitant.
    • Option 5: Last survivor Lifetime Income with 50% annuity to Surviving Spouse : Annuity amount shall be payable throughout lifetime of the Primary Annuitant and upon his / her death 50% of the initial annuity payout will be made throughout the lifetime of the Secondary Annuitant / Surviving Spouse. If the Secondary Annuitant predeceases the Primary Annuitant, 100% of the annuity payouts shall continue for the Primary Annuitant.
    • Option 6: Last survivor Lifetime Income with 100% annuity to the Surviving Spouse and Cash-back on death of Surviving Spouse : Annuity amount shall be payable throughout lifetime of the Primary Annuitant and upon his / her death 100% of the initial income payout will be made throughout the lifetime of the Secondary Annuitant / Surviving Spouse. If the Secondary Annuitant predeceases the Primary Annuitant, the annuity payments shall continue for the Primary Annuitant. On death of the surviving Annuitant, Total Premium (excluding Goods and Services Tax or other statutory levies, if any) paid for purchasing the plan will be paid back to the nominee
  5. There is no maturity benefit and death benefit other than what is already mentioned under Point 3
  6. For NPS subscribers, only Monthly Mode of Annuity Payout shall be applicable. The final annuity amount payable, will be based on the amount transferred by the NPS authority to Kotak Mahindra Life Insurance Company Ltd, so the Benefit Illustration & Proposal Form will be updated accordingly.
