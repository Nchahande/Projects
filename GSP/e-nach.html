How do I register for eNACH?
What Is e-NACH & How To Register For It?

e-NACH is set up to make it convenient for businesses and customers to handle all recurring payments like telephone bills, insurance premiums, utility bills, SIPs, school fees etc. It was set up by the National Payment Corporation of India (NPCI) to introduce best practices in electronic transactions.

All you need to do is to complete a one-time authentication for registration of e-NACH with your Net Banking or Debit Card after which all your subsequent payments will be auto-debited.


What are the benefits of e-NACH?

  1. Convenient & hassle-free: Once the customer registers for e-NACH, Kotak Life will debit the relevant amount from the approved account/card during the payment due date without having to send payment reminders.

  2. Trusted process: As the e-NACH has been set up by the National Payments Corporation of India and is regulated by the RBI under the Payment Settlement Systems Act, 2007; the e-NACH process is a fully secured and safe process for payment processing

  3. No risk of missing payment due date: As the payment is auto-debited every month, there is no worry of the policy getting lapsed because of missing the payment due date
